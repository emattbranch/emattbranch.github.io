Areas of Interest

At the University of Baltimore I have focused on advanced topics in neurodesign and research methods, as well as studying practical courses in service design, programming for UX and sequential visualization. Additionally in this program I completed the fundamental UX design courses including interaction design, information architecture, and human-computer cognition.

I am especially interested in user research which involves collecting data to create design deliverables. I am drawn towards accessibility, usability, and content organization strategies, and I am in my element performing research studies and developing key insights.

Outside of research and design I enjoy instructing the martial art Aikido, creating music, and crocheting with my cat's supervision.
